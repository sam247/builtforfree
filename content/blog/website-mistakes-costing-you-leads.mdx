Most underperforming websites have the same patterns.

## Top conversion blockers

- No clear headline explaining who you help
- Weak offer details
- Missing trust proof near call-to-action buttons
- Slow mobile experience
- Forms asking for too much information too early
- No location context for local buyers
- No follow-up system after form submission

## Quick wins this week

Start with your homepage headline, add a trust bar, and simplify your form fields to essentials.
