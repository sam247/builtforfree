A free website build means no upfront design and development fee.

## Typical ongoing costs

- Hosting from GBP 15.99/month
- Domain renewal around GBP 10/year

## What to confirm before choosing a provider

- Scope of included pages
- Revision policy
- Launch timeline
- Post-launch support terms
