Most local customers discover businesses on mobile first.

## Mobile conversion priorities

- Fast loading above-the-fold content
- Click-to-call and quick enquiry buttons
- Clear service summaries and trust indicators

If your mobile experience is weak, your lead volume usually suffers even when traffic is strong.
